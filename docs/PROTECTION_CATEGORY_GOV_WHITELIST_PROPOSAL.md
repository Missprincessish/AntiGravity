# Guru Kai Tools: Whitelists for Legal & Systems (and Safety) — Verifiable, Cited Information

**Purpose:** Each tool’s whitelist is chosen so the program can answer **all types of questions** about that topic with **verifiable, cited** information. Sources include U.S. government (`.gov`), U.S. 501(c) nonprofits, and (where useful) academically credited resources.

**Base list:** Your Guru tools list (100 tools). The tools below are the **Legal & Systems**–related ones, with the requested additions merged in and each given its **own** whitelist.

---

## Your original Legal & Systems tools (Guru items 41–50)

- 41. Court Basics: What to wear and how to address a judge.
- 42. Public Records: How to check if you have old tickets or a record.
- 43. Jury Duty: What that letter means and how to respond.
- 44. The Housing Office: How to talk to the Housing Authority without stress.
- 45. Applying for Disability: The first 3 steps if you are too sick to work.
- **46. Legal Aid:** How to find a lawyer who works for free in your area. *(expanded below)*
- **47. Child Support Systems:** How the office works and keeping your papers organized. *(expanded below)*
- 48. Unemployment: How to file the moment you lose your job.
- 49. Fixing Mistakes: How to "dispute" a bill or a government decision.
- 50. Notary Public: What a "Notary" is and why you need one for big papers.

**Additions** (from the combined list): Know Your Rights, Domestic Violence, Consumer Complaint, Workplace Discrimination, Immigration & Citizenship, Victim Compensation, Voting Rights & How to Register, Housing Discrimination, Restraining Order (all genders/sexes), Elder Abuse, Identity Theft, Small Claims, Civil Rights, **Record Expungement**.

**From Guru 41–50 also included as separate tools:** Court Basics (#41), Public Records (#42), Jury Duty (#43), Notary Public (#50). **Total: 20 tools** for this category.
